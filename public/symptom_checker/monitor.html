COVID-19 Symptom Checker


Recommendation: Monitor for symptoms from home


Monitor for symptoms of cough, fever over 100.4°F or shortness of breath at home. If you do not have symptoms, you do not need to be tested for COVID-19. Whether or not you are tested, treatment is the same.


If you develop symptoms:

  • If you develop a cough, fever over 100.4°F or shortness of breath, contact your primary care provider to determine if you should be tested.

To lower your risk of COVID-19 exposure:

  • Practice social distancing. Stay at least 6 feet away from others and avoid contact with people who may be infected. Avoid leaving the house unnecessarily.
  • Wash your hands often with soap and water. Use hand sanitizers with an alcohol level of 60% or greater if soap and water is not available.
  • Avoid touching your face.
  • Cough/sneeze into your elbow.
  • Use disinfectant to clean objects and surfaces that you frequently touch.
  • Stay informed. Visit the Simspons News Site for the most current information on updated recommended measures of protection.
  • Come back and retake the COVID-19 symptom checker if you develop new or worsening symptoms.
